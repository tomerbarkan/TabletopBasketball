Tabletop Basketball

Relive the good old physical experience - on your mobile

Digitized version of the retro tabletop basketball game, now on your Android device.

Challenge your friends face to face on the same device, in this faced paced addictive basketball game.
Practice agains the AI and then beat your friends.

Unique graphics and five different game modes make this game a delightful nostalgic experience for all ages.

Features
  • Play with friends on the same device, or practice against 3 levels of AI.
  • Great game for parties or social events.
  • 5 completely different game modes with unique controls requiring different skills to master.
  • Physics based to provide a realistic experience.
  • Unique graphics.
